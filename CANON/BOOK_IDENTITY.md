## The Death (One Sentence)
A chronic snorer is suffocated with a pillow by a sleep-deprived roommate who has been pushed past their breaking point after days without rest.

## Tagline
"Some noises you learn to live with. Some you die from."

## Themes
1. The breaking point of communal living - satirizes the dark thoughts everyone has about inconsiderate hostel neighbors
2. Sleep deprivation as a form of torture
3. The thin veneer of civilization in shared spaces
